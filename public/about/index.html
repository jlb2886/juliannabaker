Hi, I'm Julianna Baker. UX research, product design, and development are passions of mine. That's why I have a degree in Human-centered Computing and a minor in Software Engineering at Rochester Institute of Technology.

Learn more about me

When people ask about me to try and learn more I usually bring up my family since they are such a large part of my life. I am one of eight kids and I am the youngest. It is my favorite fact about myself but if you want to know a little more keep reading. I love to hike, backpack, ski, snowboard, water ski, wake board, skateboard, and long board. Yes I know that is a lot, I just have the personality that will try everything and my determination does not let me fail.

Aside from all the fun I have outside the office I have a passion for accessibility in technology and design as well as UX research. I believe that everyone should have access to all kinds of software so with my concentration in accessibility I am able to help make that happen. I believe that accessibility is one of the biggest parts of UX design and Product Design. When software is more accessible it usually has a better user experience.
